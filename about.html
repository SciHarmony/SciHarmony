About

SciHarmony is a student-led nonprofit making inclusive dental STEM: sensory-aware Clinic-Prep tools for first-time visits and hands-on Ortho Lab sessions that teach brackets, wires, and forces. We publish everything open-source so schools and clinics can reuse it.

What we do

  • Clinic-Prep: visual schedules, AAC dental boards, quiet-hour checklists, and low-volume tool demos with caregivers present.
  • Ortho Lab: pegboard “teeth,” elastics, spring scales, and wire samples to predict → measure → reduce error.
  • Access Kits: a short on-ramp of calm STEM activities to recruit and train mentors for the dental tracks.

Our guardrails

We provide education and preparation only. Any clinical services (screenings, varnish, fittings) are performed by licensed providers under their consent and paperwork. We use people-first, respectful language and never share identifying info.

Partners

We co-design with an orthodontist or pediatric dentist, a special-education/OT or child-life specialist, and a school or library site lead.

How we measure impact

  • Preparedness (0–5) before and after Clinic-Prep workshops
  • Mirror-tolerance seconds (best of three attempts)
  • Force prediction error (RMSE) in Ortho Lab sessions
  • Sites, sessions, students served, volunteers trained, and cost per student
