About SciHarmony

SciHarmony is a student-led nonprofit focused on inclusive dental STEM: sensory-aware Clinic-Prep for first-time visits and hands-on Ortho Lab sessions that teach brackets, wires, and forces. We publish our materials open-source so schools and clinics can reuse them.

What we do

  • Clinic-Prep: visual schedules, AAC dental boards, quiet-hour checklists, and low-volume tool demos.
  • Ortho Lab: pegboard teeth, elastics, spring scales; predict → measure → reduce error.
  • STEM Access Kits: calm on-ramp activities that feed into our dental tracks.

Guardrails

Education and preparation only. Any clinical services are performed by licensed providers with their consent and paperwork.
