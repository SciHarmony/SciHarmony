Get Involved

Three ways to help inclusive dental STEM spread without chaos.

Volunteer

  • Facilitator — run Clinic-Prep stations (mirror, suction sound, visual schedule) or Ortho Lab rigs.
  • Design — help update visual cards, AAC boards, and lab sheets.
  • Data — log preparedness scores, tolerance seconds, and force readings.

Email hello@sciharmony.org with the subject “Volunteer.”

Partner

Schools, libraries, and clinics: host a workshop, quiet-hour tools tour, or a 3-session Ortho Lab. We bring materials and training.

  • Clinic-Prep: 60 min, caregivers attend
  • Ortho Lab: 60 min, 12–24 students, 4 rigs
  • We share a one-page report after each event

Email hello@sciharmony.org with the subject “Partner.”

Donate

$10 funds one Clinic-Prep kit; $180 buys a full Ortho Lab rig. Sponsors appear on our site and receive an impact snapshot.
